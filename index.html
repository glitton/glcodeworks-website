about

After graduating with a BS in Electronics Engineering, I started my career as an audio engineer. Wanting to gain business skills, I completed an MBA from the University of San Francisco. This along with my technical skills enabled me to have a Silicon Valley career in product and solutions marketing for databases and data storage software. During this time, I continued to investigate web development. It was not until early 2016 that I dove deeper into HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python and started building apps from scratch. By 2017, I followed my passion and trained to be a full-time web developer.

Currently, I am a freelance web developer as well as a solutions and customer success engineer for various start-up companies. I use my technical skills along with my business acumen to help my customers to get the most out of the solutions that I represent whether it be optimizing front-end performance or mitigating issues before it impacts the business.

On a side note, I own a vacation rental in Puerto Morelos, Mexico. Feel free to check it out here!
